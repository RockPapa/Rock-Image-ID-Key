Rock Image ID Privacy Policy

Rock Image ID is an educational rock-identification and reference app. The app does not collect, store, sell, or share your personal information.

Information we collect

Rock Image ID does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photographs, or other personal information. The app does not include developer-operated analytics, advertising, tracking, or profiling technologies.

Device permissions

Rock Image ID does not request access to your camera, photo library, precise location, microphone, contacts, health information, or advertising identifier.

Offline content

The core identification key, rock index, descriptions, and reference images are included in the app and can be used without an internet connection. Your choices within the identification key are processed on your device and are not transmitted to the developer.

Optional external websites

Some optional reference buttons open content supplied by third parties, including Wikipedia, Google, and Rockd. When you choose to visit an external website, that provider may receive standard internet information such as your IP address, browser or device information, and cookies. Their handling of that information is governed by their own privacy policies, not this policy.

  • Google Privacy Policy

Data retention and deletion

Because the developer does not collect or store personal information through Rock Image ID, there is no developer-held personal data to retain or delete. Data stored by an external website is subject to that provider’s policies and controls.

Children’s privacy

Rock Image ID is educational and does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults. Optional external websites may have their own age requirements and privacy practices.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if the app’s features or data practices change. The current version will be posted at this location with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact

Questions about Rock Image ID or this privacy policy may be submitted through the project’s GitHub support page.
